### One-liners
- "Spotify for finding people, not playlists."
- "The social layer music has always needed."
- "Your listening history, finally doing something social."

### Longer pitch paragraph
"Music taste is the most honest signal of who you are. It's not curated like an Instagram grid. It's not performative like a Twitter bio. It's what you play at 2am when no one's watching. Tunect reads that data and finds you the people who listen the way you do — ranked by how deep the overlap actually goes. We're building the social layer that music has always needed but never had."

### Investor one-pager headline
"The world's first social platform built entirely on music identity."

### Research citations (for pitch/landing)
- "Shared music preferences create interpersonal bonds because music taste signals similar value orientations — and similarity in values drives social attraction." — *Personality and Social Psychology Bulletin, Boer et al.*
- "Music taste can act as an honest signal of the group we belong to, revealing where and when we grew up." — *University of Oxford, Department of Experimental Psychology*
- "Listening to music increases levels of cooperation, interaction, and conversation." — *Research in Psychotherapy, 2024*
